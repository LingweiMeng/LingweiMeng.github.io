About

I'm currently a Ph.D. student at Human-Computer Communications Laboratory (HCCL), The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Helen Meng. My research interests focus on speech processing and recognition.

Before that, I received my M.Phil. degree from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), supervised by Prof. Jie Tian and Prof. Di Dong, working on medical image analysis. And I received my Bachelor's degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) as an Outstanding Graduate.
