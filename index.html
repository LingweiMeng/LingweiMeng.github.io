About

I'm a Ph.D. candidate from Human-Computer Communications Laboratory (HCCL), The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Helen Meng. My research interests focus on speech processing and recognition.

Before that, I received my M.Phil. degree from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), supervised by Prof. Jie Tian, working on medical image analysis. I received my Bachelor's degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) as an Outstanding Graduate.
